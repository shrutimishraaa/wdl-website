HoD's Desk

Department of Electronics and Telecommunications was established in the year 1994. Presently the intake of students for the U.G course is 120 in the morning shift and 60 in the afternoon shift which is exclusively for diploma students .Post graduate course was inducted in the year 2002 with an intake of 18.

The department’s vision is to nurture students to become technocrats with a humane outlook. Department’s strength lies in the well qualified, intelligent and diligent faculty. The laboratories are equipped with the state of art hardware and software so that our students are trained effectively and are able to apply their acquired knowledge and skills to solve real life technical challenges.

Many of our alumni are industry captains, business leaders, entrepreneurs and members of research organizations globally .In a nutshell the department is well nurtured and progressive to cater to all needs and aspirations of our students.

Vision:

  1. Towards developing a centre of excellence in the field of Electronics and Telecommunication and to nurture students to become technocrats with a humane outlook.

Mission:

  1. To empower the budding engineers to meet the growing challenges of industry.
  2. To promote a cutting-edge research to benefit the society locally and globally.
  3. To develop young engineers with human and social intellectual qualities required for practicing as responsible engineers.
